## 💰 Cost Management

### Staying Within Free Tier

**AWS** (12 months):

- EC2: 750 hours/month (t2.micro)
- S3: 5GB
- RDS: 750 hours/month
- Lambda: 1M requests/month

**GCP** ($300 credit for 90 days):

- Use credit wisely
- Always free tier after credit expires
- f1-micro instance always free

**Azure** ($200 credit for 30 days):

- Use credit for learning
- 12 months free tier after
- B1S VM for 750 hours/month

**IBM Cloud** (Lite - Forever):

- Limited but free forever
- No credit card needed
- Good for basic learning

### Cost Saving Tips

1. ✅ Always delete resources after practice
2. ✅ Set billing alerts at $5, $10, $20
3. ✅ Use smallest instance types
4. ✅ Stop instances when not in use
5. ✅ Monitor usage daily
6. ✅ Use spot/preemptible instances
7. ✅ Clean up old snapshots/backups
